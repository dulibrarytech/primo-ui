What is Compass?

Compass provides a simple, one-step way to search the vast majority of our collections and databases. For more targeted searching, try searching individual databases, which contain subject-specific resources with more search options.

Sign into your library account to renew materials, save items and searches to your e-Shelf, and more.

What is Compass searching?

There are 4 options for scoping your search in Compass. After performing a search, you can choose one from the search drop down.

Catalog : Print Books, some E-Books, Journal Titles, some Database Titles, Archival Materials, DVDs, Music Scores, Video Games

Articles+ : Journal Articles, Newspaper Articles, Reviews, Dissertations, some E-Books

Search All : Searches all of the above (Catalog & Articles+ combined)

Course Reserves : Searches items on course reserve. You can limit by course number or instructor after performing the initial search.
